## What is Channel3?

Channel3 lets developers build AI shopping apps and agents. Whether you're creating a smart recommendation engine, a visual search tool, an agent that purchases products autononmously, or something totally new, Channel3 can power it.

## What can you do with Channel3?

### Find any product on the internet

Search for furniture, fashion, electronics — whatever you can think of – with text, images, or both.

<Note>We're starting with physical products in the U.S.</Note>

### Earn commission on sales

We've partnered with tens of thousands of brands who pay us when we drive them sales. We take a cut and pay you the rest.
